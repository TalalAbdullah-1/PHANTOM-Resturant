About Us

Our story of culinary excellence

Our Story

Founded in 2020, PHANTOM emerged from a vision to create an extraordinary dining experience where innovation meets tradition. Our journey began with a simple belief: exceptional food requires exceptional ingredients, meticulous preparation, and passionate presentation.

Today, PHANTOM stands as a beacon of culinary excellence, blending modern techniques with time-honored recipes. Our menu celebrates seasonal ingredients sourced from local farmers and artisans who share our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Marcus Belmont

Executive Chef

With over 20 years of culinary experience across three continents, Chef Marcus brings a wealth of knowledge and creativity to PHANTOM. Trained in Paris at Le Cordon Bleu, he worked under renowned chefs in Michelin-starred restaurants before establishing his unique culinary vision here.

His philosophy centers on respecting ingredients, honoring their natural flavors, and presenting them in unexpected, delightful ways. Each dish tells a story, inviting guests on a memorable gastronomic journey.
